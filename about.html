About ULK Student Hostel

A purpose-built home for ULK University students — safe, affordable, and connected to everything you need to succeed.

Who We Are

ULK Student Hostel was created with a single purpose: to give ULK University students a comfortable, safe, and affordable place to call home during their studies. We understand that student life is demanding — between lectures, assignments, and exams, the last thing you need to worry about is where you live.

Our hostel is strategically located near ULK campus, eliminating long commutes and giving you more time for what matters — your education.
